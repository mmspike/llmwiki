# Nozomi Networks

---

## Overview
**Nozomi Networks** is a **leading provider of OT (Operational Technology) and IoT (Internet of Things) security solutions**, specializing in protecting **industrial control systems (ICS)**, **critical infrastructure**, and **enterprise OT environments**. Founded in **2013**, Nozomi Networks delivers **real-time visibility, threat detection, and asset management** for industries such as energy, manufacturing, transportation, and utilities.

---

## Key Offerings
1. **Guardian**: A platform for **real-time monitoring, threat detection, and asset visibility** in OT and ICS networks. It provides deep insights into industrial processes and detects anomalies that may indicate cyber threats.
2. **Central Management Console (CMC)**: A centralized management interface for **configuring, monitoring, and managing** multiple Guardian instances across an organization.
3. **Vantage**: A cloud-based or on-premises platform that aggregates data from Guardian and other sources to provide **enterprise-wide OT security visibility**.
4. **Lens**: A **threat intelligence and analytics** platform that helps organizations understand and respond to cyber threats in OT environments.

---

## Industries Served
Nozomi Networks' solutions are designed to secure a wide range of industries, including:
- **Energy and Utilities**: Power plants, oil and gas, water treatment facilities.
- **Manufacturing**: Automotive, pharmaceutical, food and beverage.
- **Transportation**: Rail, aviation, maritime.
- **Critical Infrastructure**: Government, healthcare, and financial sectors.

## Security Research and Transparency
Nozomi Networks maintains a **Product Security Incident Response Team (PSIRT)** dedicated to identifying, investigating, and mitigating vulnerabilities in its products. The company regularly publishes **security bulletins** to inform customers and the public about vulnerabilities and their remediations.

### Notable Security Bulletins
- [[NN-2019:1-01 - Stored XSS in field name data model]]: A **Stored XSS** vulnerability in Guardian/CMC, affecting versions before **v19.0.4**.
- [[NN-2026:5-01 - HTML injection in Users in Guardian/CMC before 26.1.0]]: HTML injection in the Users functionality, affecting versions before **v26.1.0**.
- [[NN-2026:6-01 - HTML injection in Schedule Restore Archive in Guardian/CMC before 26.1.0]]: HTML injection in the Schedule Restore Archive functionality, affecting versions before **v26.1.0**.
- [[NN-2026:7-01 - HTML injection in Smart Polling in Guardian/CMC before 26.1.0]]: HTML injection in Smart Polling, affecting versions before **v26.1.0**.

## Commitment to Security
Nozomi Networks is committed to **proactively addressing security vulnerabilities** in its products. The company collaborates with **external researchers** and **internal teams** to identify and mitigate vulnerabilities. Customers are encouraged to:
1. **Upgrade to the latest versions** of Nozomi Networks products to mitigate known vulnerabilities.
2. **Monitor security bulletins** for updates and remediation guidance.
3. **Report vulnerabilities** to the [Nozomi Networks PSIRT](https://security.nozominetworks.com/psirt/).
